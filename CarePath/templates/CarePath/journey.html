Your Journey

1. MDM Meeting

The Multidisciplinary Meeting (MDM) is a special clinic where a team of dedicated health professionals from different areas come together to discuss and review cases involving head and neck issues.

Read More

2. Dental Assessment

A dental assessment and treatments before your cancer treatments are crucial steps in your cancer care journey. By addressing dental issues early, we can prevent complications, reduce the risk of serious conditions like osteoradionecrosis, and maintain your oral health.

Read More

3. Possible Treatments and Side effects

Most head and neck cancers are treated with surgery, radiotherapy, chemotherapy or a combination of these modalities.

Read More

4. Oral Mucositis and Dry Mouth Support Clinic

This clinic is available to patients receiving radiation treatment to the oral cavity or mouth. It runs simultaneously with your radiotherapy treatment and occurs each Thursday morning between 8.30 am to 12 pm.

Read More

5. Post-treatment Dental Visits

After your treatment, you will soon have an appointment with our dental team, where we'll examine your mouth and address any remaining dental needs.

Read More

6. Discharge

As you transition back to the community, our dental team will discharge you from our service. We will send a discharge letter to your GP detailing the treatment you received while under our care.
