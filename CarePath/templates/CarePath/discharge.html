Discharge Information



Congratulations on completing your treatment! As you transition back to the community, our dental team will discharge you from our service. We will send a discharge letter to your GP detailing the treatment you received while under our care.


Avoid Tooth Extractions

It's important to remember that if you need any dental work in the future, you must inform your private dentist that you have had radiotherapy in the head and neck region. This information is crucial because radiotherapy can affect how your gums and bones heal. By sharing this detail, you can help prevent complications and avoid unnecessary tooth extractions.


Ongoing Oral Health Care

Taking care of your oral health is vital for your overall well-being. Regular dental visits and following the advice given will help you maintain a healthy mouth and prevent future issues.


Congratulations again on completing your head and neck cancer treatment. We wish you continued good health and happiness on your journey forward. If you have any questions or need further assistance, don't hesitate to reach out.
